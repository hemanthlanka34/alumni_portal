Student Alumni portal of raghu educational institutes em-place in endeavor to bring all alumni on one platform to create a group of raghu alumni and to build long lasting relations. So, the main aim to develop this web-based application is that is going to make it possible for the former old and new students of the college to keep in touch with each other and that will substitute the manual system of the alumni office with an automated one.
